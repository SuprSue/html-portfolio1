The Best Movies of All Time According to Sue

These are my top 3 movies of all time

Twister

This is a great action packed movie with before it's time special effects.

My Dog Skip

This movie is a coming of age film that helps you learn how to deal with life and death.

Christmas Vacation

A wonderful movie to start a family tradition plus who does not love Chevy Chase?


These are my top 3 series of all time

Little House on the Prairie

By far the best wholesome series out there.

Heartland

Modern setting with a Little House feel.

The Walking Dead

I have always been a fan of zombie movies. This series is well done and the special
effects are so good. The make up artists are excellent as well.
