About Me

My dream career would be to use my new skills to develop new projects for a government funded company. I would love to work on something that would benefit veterans. As a veteran myself I would welcome the opportunity to help those reentering civilian life.

I know I do not look like the typical new developer fresh out of college, but I feel my previous skills allow me to see things in a whole different light. I love keeping up on new technology and learning new skills. I am very well organized and respectful of time. I have worked well with a variety of people with all types of personalities.
